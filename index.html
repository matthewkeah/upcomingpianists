Why Cadenza?

Intimate Cohorts

Masterclasses are strictly capped at 6 active performers per session to ensure individualized, highly technical feedback on posture, wrist weight, and phrasing.

Concert Pianos

Technique cannot be refined on compromised actions. All live portal sessions are conducted on fully regulated, concert-prepped Steinway Model D grand pianos.

Advanced Repertoire

Dedicated stylistic tracks covering everything from the intricate counterpoint of J.S. Bach to the sweeping, polyrhythmic demands of Rachmaninoff.
