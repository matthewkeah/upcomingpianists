Fixed Monthly Anchor

The Masterclass Format

Sessions are fixed to the second Saturday of each month. Consistency of date matters more than flexibility. People plan around predictable schedules.

Standard 2.5-Hour Agenda

10 Minutes
Opening Check-In

Brief community welcome, organizational announcements, and introduction of the monthly repertoire theme.

90 Minutes
Repertoire Presentations

2 to 3 members present 1 to 2 pieces each. Allocated 15-20 minutes per slot including active peer critique.

20 Minutes
Structured Critique & Synthesis

Open floor for broader technical reflection on phrasing, wrist weight, and interpretation heard across all performances.

30 Minutes
Planning & Informal Close

Confirm next session presenters, followed by open social time and optional jamming.

Hybrid by Default

Every live session features an active video link (Google Meet / Zoom). This enables remote founders (e.g., Musila in the US) and traveling members to participate seamlessly.

Presentation Guidelines:
  • A rotating schedule ensures every Active Member presents at least once per quarter.
  • No individual presents two sessions in a row unless open slots remain unfilled.
  • Remote presenters may submit high-res audio/video recordings with prior notice.
