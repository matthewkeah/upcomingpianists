Internal Communications

We utilize a deliberate, low-noise communication strategy to ensure messages are addressed rather than ignored.

The Two-Group System

The Leaders Group

A strict decision-making space. Every message must name the person it requires a response from. Silence is not consent. Agendas are posted here 3 weeks prior to any session.

The General Hub

The public face of the community. We limit broadcast-style administrative announcements and use this space strictly for repertoire celebration, session highlights, and constructive artistic discourse.

Frequently Asked Questions

Attendance is a trust signal, not a punishment system. If you miss two consecutive sessions, the People's Coordinator will check in. Missing four moves you to Associate status. There is no shame in stepping back when life gets busy.

Currently, there are no formal financial structures. A monthly dues system will only be introduced once the group successfully maintains 3 consecutive months of high-value active sessions.

KCPO operates on distributed ownership. We have a Convener, not a boss. Leadership roles are rotational and no role is held for more than one year without a renewal vote from the active core.
