Organizational Framework

Founded by pianists, for pianists. Formalizing a structure that honors our origins in Nairobi while equipping us for a sustainable future.

Our Core Values

Consistency

We show up, even when it is inconvenient. Predictability of schedule builds trust.

Honest Critique

We give mutual peer feedback that is truthful, technically specific, and kind.

Shared Ownership

Every active member is a stakeholder and driver, not a passive passenger.

Artistic Ambition

We aim higher together toward public recitals than we would practice alone.

Distributed Leadership Structure

To prevent burnout, roles are rotational and held for a maximum of one year without a renewal vote.

Role Core Responsibilities Accountability Metric
Chairperson / Convener Facilitates live sessions, holds group vision, acts as engine rather than boss. Elected annually by active members. Remote participation permitted.
Masterclass Coordinator Schedules sessions, confirms presenters, manages venue logistics. Sends monthly schedule at least 3 weeks in advance.
People's Coordinator Member welfare, onboarding, tracks attendance trends. Flags disengagement early and conducts personal 1-on-1 outreach.
Secretary / Scribe Documents session repertoire, records formal decisions. Circulates written summary notes within 48 hours of meeting.
