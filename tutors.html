Executive Faculty & Core Layer

The dedicated peers responsible for driving organizational momentum, repertoire curation, and monthly masterclass hosting[cite: 1, 3, 4].

Governance & Voting Eligibility

Active Core Maintain $\ge75\%$ attendance at monthly anchor sessions. Hold formal voting rights on framework amendments and rotational leadership elections[cite: 3].
Associate Layer Participate occasionally due to academic or professional constraints. Fully welcome to present repertoire and engage in peer critique[cite: 3].
Honorary / Advisors Recognised founding contributors residing abroad. Consulted on high-level artistic direction and formal registration milestones[cite: 3].
